  • Despite the vast numbers of communication channels, people grow lonely without having real contact to other human beings. Since interactions get oversimplified, we lose the necessity to foster our social contacts outside the digital world. S'bickt is an attempt, which lets the digital and the real world become symbiotic partners, to counteract against the mentioned progress. Our app aims at raising and satisfying the necessity for nature and outdoor activities. We offer a innovative platform for communication, activity promotion and games.

s'bickt (austrian dialekt for "it sticks") is an augmented reality, community based app available for iOS and Android devices. It tags your messages with geodata and projects them onto the screen, when you activate the camera.
